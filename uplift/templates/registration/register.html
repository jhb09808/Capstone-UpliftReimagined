Volunteer

Three Ways to Get Involved

  1. Volunteer on our Uplift Routes

    Help deliver meals, clothing and necessities to the homeless. Uplift trucks 'roll out' at 6pm on Mondays and Wednesdays; 5pm on Saturdays. The average route takes 4 – 5 hours. Please call in advance to schedule your participation.

    Contact: Amy Cox 913-731-6046 or Email volunteer@uplift.org

    Volunteering on the routes for the first time? Click HERE to view guidelines and expectations.

    Our address is 1516 Prospect, Kansas City, MO 64127. There are three large white overhead doors in the alley for drop off. If the doors are closed, just knock on them or honk.

  2. Prepare Meals

    Cooks are needed to prepare and deliver meals to Uplift for approximately 60 people.

    Click on the link for recipe and instructions. Cook's Manual

    Contact: Renee Jones 816-377-6009 hiampacity@yahoo.com

  3. Warehouse Work Sessions

    Every Saturday 9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.

    Come help us sort donations, stock our shelves, fold clothes and have a good old time doing it.

    This is an excellent service opportunity for elementary/high school students, youth sports teams and other organized groups.

    Register Now!

Volunteer Coordinator Email: volunteer@uplift.org

General Email: info@uplift.org

Uplift Warehouse ADDRESS
1516 Prospect Ave.
Kansas City, MO 64127

(please do not ship parcels or packages to the warehouse)
